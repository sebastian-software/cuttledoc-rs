sidecar and attribution record.

The attribution record identifies the exact article revision, contributor
history, transformations, TTS model, runtime, and voice configuration.

CC BY-SA permits redistribution and adaptation, including commercial use,
provided its attribution, change-indication, license-link, and ShareAlike
conditions are followed. Consumers who copy these assets are responsible for
preserving those notices. This repository policy deliberately does not apply a

terms require it; model and reference-voice dependencies are still recorded
separately in provenance.
